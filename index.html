Ghana

Ghana is country in the western part of Africa, sharing border to the East with Togo, to the west with Ivory Coast, to the north with Burkina Faso, and the Gulf of Guinea to the south. It is the world's second largest exporter of cocoa and has many tourist sites including wildlife and beaches.
